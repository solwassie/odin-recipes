Lasagna's ingredients

  • Meat: a pound of beef and half a pound of sausage.
  • An onion and two cloves of garlic.
  • A can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Two tablespoons of sugar.
  • Spices and seasonings.
  • Lasagna noodles.
  • Cheese: parmesan, mozzarella, ricotta.
  • An egg.

Steps to make a lasagna

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna following a pattern.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest bofore serving.
